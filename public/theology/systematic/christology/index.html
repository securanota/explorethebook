The Doctrine of Christ

The Doctrine of Christ (Christology)#

Introduction#

Christology is the study of the person and work of Jesus Christ—who He is and what He accomplished for our salvation.

Core Articles in This Section#

The Person of Christ#

  • The Incarnation - God becoming man
  • Two Natures in One Person - Fully God and fully human
  • The Sinlessness of Christ - Jesus’ moral perfection
  • Christ as Prophet, Priest, and King - Jesus’ official roles

The Work of Christ#

  • The Atonement - Christ’s sacrifice for sin
  • Theories of Atonement - Different understandings of the cross
  • The Resurrection - Christ’s victory over death
  • The Ascension and Session - Christ’s exaltation
  • The Second Coming - Christ’s promised return

Christ in Biblical Theology#

  • Old Testament Christology - Messianic prophecies and types
  • Christ as Second Adam - Representing humanity
  • Christ as Mediator - Bridging God and humanity

Study Sequence#

For systematic study, we recommend this order:

  1. The Incarnation (who Christ is)
  2. Two Natures in One Person (how deity and humanity unite)
  3. The Atonement (what Christ accomplished)
  4. The Resurrection (validation of His work)
  5. Ascension and Session (Christ’s present ministry)

  • Hope: Confidence in Christ’s return
  • Mission: Proclaiming the unique Savior
